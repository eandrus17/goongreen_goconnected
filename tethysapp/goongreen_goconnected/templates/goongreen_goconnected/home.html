Help

This application calculates a connectivity rating for cities in Utah and Salt Lake Counties. There are three measures used to calculate the score: elevation change, intersection density, and number of bus stops.

Start on the Map Page.

To calculate the elevation change, intersection density, and number of bus stops, select a city from the drop-down menu, then click Submit.

Then, to see the connectivity rating for each measure and the overall connectivity score, click the Show Me the $$$ button.

To export the Results Page to a PDF, click the Export to PDF button, and it will save as Connectivity_Results.pdf.
